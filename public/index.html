Commands

Underlined commands are DJ only

.play plays tracks from youtube with given query/url, Search for a track on YouTube
.join Joins bot to your current voice channel.
.leave Disconnects bot from your voice channel
.queue Shows the Tracks Listed in queue(shows max 10 results)
.skip Skips the current playing track
.remove Removes track from the queue at given postion
.stop Stops the player and clears the queue
.fwd Forwards by a certain amount in the current track
.ping Shows bot ping
.search Improved search result, use only when .play doesn't show correct result
.donate Link to patreon page
.help Shows list of commands with bot invitation link, it also displays command info

How do I restrict music commands to a specific channel?

The bot will only listen to music commands invoked in channels it can actually write to. Simply override it such that the music bot can only talk in the channels you want the bot to be usable from. If you want to restrict the bot to a specific role, you can always just restrict the bot to a channel only accessible by a specific set of roles

The bot is responding, but I can't hear it play music?
